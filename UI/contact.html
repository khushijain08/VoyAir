Frequently Asked Questions

How can I modify my booking?

You can modify your booking online through your account dashboard or contact our reservations team at +1 (800) 123-4568.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy varies by fare type. Most tickets can be cancelled with a refund minus applicable fees. Check your booking details for specific terms.

How do I check-in online?

Online check-in opens 24 hours before departure. Simply log into your account and follow the check-in prompts.

Can I change my seat after booking?

Yes, you can change your seat selection through your booking management page or by contacting customer service.
